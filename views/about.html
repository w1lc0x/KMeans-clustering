About Aggrey Consultants

Aggrey Consultants, is a local based construction services company and is a leading builder in diverse market segments.

The company has earned recognition for undertaking big projects, fostering innovation, embracing emerging technologies, and making a difference for their clients, employees and community.

With a staff of 500 employees, the company completes 10 million of construction on 500 projects each year. Aggrey offers clients the accessibility and support of a local firm with the stability and resources of a multi-national organization.
